## Overview

The experience of listening to music on a computer has historically been compromised by the audio processing chain that stands between a digital audio file and the listener's headphones or speakers — sample rate conversion, volume normalization, mixing with system sounds, and other operations that subtly degrade the audio quality relative to the original recording. <a href="#">VOX</a> was built specifically to eliminate these compromises for listeners who care about audio fidelity, providing a music player that handles the full resolution of lossless audio files with <a href="#">bit-perfect delivery</a> to compatible audio hardware. The application bypasses the standard macOS audio mixer when outputting to hardware DACs and audiophile headphone amplifiers, ensuring that the digital audio signal reaches the conversion stage unmodified and at the full precision of the source file. For listeners using quality audio hardware, this bypass mode represents a meaningful, audible improvement over standard audio playback.

The format support in <a href="#">VOX</a> covers the full spectrum of high-resolution audio standards that the audiophile community values. FLAC and ALAC files at resolutions up to 32-bit/768kHz are played with complete fidelity, while <a href="#">DSD (Direct Stream Digital)</a> files — the native format of SACD recordings — are handled through both DSD-native passthrough for compatible hardware and high-quality PCM conversion for standard DACs. The application correctly manages the sample rate switching required when transitioning between files recorded at different resolutions, a detail that basic audio players often handle poorly and that can introduce brief artifacts or silence between tracks. The <a href="#">gapless playback</a> system ensures that live albums, continuous DJ mixes, and classical works with connected movements play without interruption.

<a href="#">VOX Cloud</a> extends the application's value by providing a personal cloud music locker service designed specifically around the needs of audiophile music collections. Unlike streaming services that transcode audio to lower quality for distribution efficiency, VOX Cloud stores your uploaded files in their <a href="#">original lossless format</a> and streams them back to your Mac or iOS devices at full quality over Wi-Fi or high-bandwidth mobile connections. This makes your high-resolution music collection accessible on any of your Apple devices without the compromises of compressed streaming services, while freeing local device storage from the burden of a large lossless music library. For audiophiles who have invested in high-quality recordings and playback hardware, <a href="#">VOX OSX</a> delivers the listening experience those investments deserve.

## Key Features

- Bit-perfect <a href="#">lossless playback</a> of FLAC, ALAC, WAV, AIFF, and DSD formats
- Support for <a href="#">Hi-Res audio</a> up to 32-bit/768kHz for audiophile-grade quality
- Integrated <a href="#">VOX Cloud</a> music locker for unlimited hi-res music storage
- Seamless <a href="#">Last.fm scrobbling</a> and music discovery integration
- Advanced <a href="#">equalizer</a> with customizable frequency bands and presets
- Direct hardware output bypassing <a href="#">macOS audio mixer</a> for bit-perfect delivery
- Lyrics display and <a href="#">SoundCloud streaming</a> integration
- Elegant <a href="#">mini player mode</a> with album art display and media controls
- Support for <a href="#">AirPlay and Bluetooth</a> output to wireless audio devices
- Comprehensive <a href="#">music library</a> management with smart playlist support

## Additional Information

VOX requires macOS 10.13 High Sierra or later and is available as a free download with optional VOX Premium subscription for cloud locker access. Available on the Mac App Store and VOX website. Apple Silicon native support ensures efficient battery usage during extended listening sessions.
